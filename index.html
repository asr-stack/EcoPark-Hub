Energy‑harvesting mezzo tiles

Each parking block at EcoCharge Hub uses layered mezzo‑electric tiles that quietly turn footsteps and vehicle movement into useful clean energy for lighting and charging.

How they work

These tiles compress slightly under weight, using piezo‑electric materials to transform that pressure into small bursts of electrical energy. At scale, across thousands of vehicle passes per day, those bursts become a dependable clean power source.

Instead of one large power plant, each tile behaves like a tiny generator. We aggregate their output to feed guidance lights, occupancy sensors, and EV chargers throughout the hub in real time.

Energy production is tracked at the block level, so visitors can see how much energy their stay helped generate and how much of their own charging session was powered by on‑site renewables.
